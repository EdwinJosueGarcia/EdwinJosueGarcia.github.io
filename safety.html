K.I.W.A.
"Our vision is to bring together workers, community members, and students in a broad, multi-ethnic coalition."-K.I.W.A.

K.I.W.A. provides support for low wage workers and fights for the rights of immigrant workers. The organization holds protest amongst Koreatown's workers fighting for the needs and equality.

Summer-Night-Lights
The Summer Night Lights' mission is to reduce gang involvement in parks. This program provides safety with law enforcement and promotes family activities such as basketball, soccer, baseball, arts and crafts, and Zumba, to name a few. Summer Night Lights program also provides food for everyone including the homeless.


Lafayette Recreation Center is like any other local park they provide equitable opportunities to families and children to stay out of any trouble. They host events such as Summer Night Lights, and HOLA all programs funded by the government with the mission to keep families and children together and bond with others.
